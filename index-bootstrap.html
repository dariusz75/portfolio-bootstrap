About

An enthusiastic and professional Junior Front End Developer, with a broad range of skills acquired in Graphic Design, Web Development and the development of promotional materials. Quick to grasp new ideas and concepts, and to develop innovative and creative solutions to problems. Able to work well on own initiative and can demonstrate the high levels of motivation required to meet the tightest of deadlines. Even under pressure, possesses a strong ability to perform effectively.

Currently looking to embark on a new and challenging career within the field of Front End Development and secure a role which would enable the use of existing skills while allowing the opportunity for continued professional development.
